There are wide variety of accommadations in Chincoteague Island, including Hotels, Bed and Breakfasts, Vacation Rental Homes, and Campgrounds. Remember that Chincoteague Island is only about seven miles long and three miles wide, so anywhere you choose stay you will be within a short drive to everything. The historic downtown area is on Main Street. Maddox Blvd leads to Assateague Island and the Beach. The Chincoteague National Wildlife Refuge is located on Assateague Island.
